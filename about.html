About Fund the Future Colorado

Our Mission

Fund the Future Colorado is dedicated to expanding educational opportunities for students across the state through strategic funding of extracurricular activities and CTSOs (Career and Technical Student Organizations). We believe that every student deserves access to enriching educational experiences that go beyond the classroom.
